Cloud-hosted Instructions:

  1. Make sure your Arduino with HC-05 is powered and transmitting data
  2. Ensure your device is on the same local network as your Arduino
  3. Click "Connect" and select your HC-05 device when prompted
  4. Allow Bluetooth permissions in your browser (Chrome/Edge recommended)
  5. Data will appear automatically once connected
